About This Site

Welcome to the Art 109 Code Challenges site! Here, you'll find a collection of programming tasks designed to help you learn and practice web development skills. Whether you're a beginner or looking to sharpen your abilities, these challenges will guide you through HTML, CSS, and JavaScript concepts.

The site is maintained by the Art 109 team and is intended as a resource for students and self-learners. Explore the challenges, try building your own solutions, and don't hesitate to reach out via the contact page if you have questions or suggestions!
