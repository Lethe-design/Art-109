Audiovisual

This video outlines my techniques for manipulating light and the environment to enhance the eerie atmosphere of the video using Adobe After Effects. I had one goal in mind: to change the lighting in order to create a more unsettling mood. Initially, I wasn't sure how to achieve this, so I started using the search bar in Adobe After Effects and experimented with various effects until I found one that worked well. And I was pleased with the results!
